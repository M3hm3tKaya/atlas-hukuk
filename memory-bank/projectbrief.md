# Atlas Hukuk - Project Brief

## Overview
Atlas Hukuk is a corporate law firm website for a prestigious Istanbul Levent-based firm. 8-page website with dark navy, gold, and white color scheme conveying trust, authority, and professionalism.

## Core Identity
- **Brand**: Atlas Hukuk Burosu (Atlas Law Office)
- **Location**: Levent, Besiktas, Istanbul
- **Founded**: 2001
- **Team**: 12 lawyers (2 founders, 4 senior, 6 junior)
- **Experience**: 25+ years

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 16 (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS v4
- Framer Motion
- GSAP + @gsap/react
- Lenis (smooth scroll)
- clsx

## Pages (8)
1. Home (/)
2. Expertise (/uzmanlik)
3. Team (/ekip)
4. Success Stories (/basari-hikayeleri)
5. Publications (/yayinlar)
6. Media (/medya)
7. Career (/kariyer)
8. Contact (/iletisim)
