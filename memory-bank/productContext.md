# Atlas Hukuk - Product Context

## Why This Product Exists
A corporate law firm needs a professional, trustworthy web presence to attract high-value clients and establish market authority in Istanbul's competitive legal landscape.

## Target Audience
- Corporate clients seeking legal counsel (M&A, commercial disputes)
- Individuals needing family, criminal, or property law assistance
- Young lawyers seeking career opportunities
- Media outlets looking for legal commentary

## Design Philosophy
- **Serious & Dignified**: Slow animations (0.8-1s), serif headings, minimal motion
- **Trust-First**: Dark navy background conveys authority and stability
- **Gold Accents Only**: Gold used sparingly for highlights, lines, badges
- **Clean & Uncluttered**: Generous whitespace, clear hierarchy

## User Experience Goals
- Convey professionalism and decades of experience
- Make it easy to find relevant expertise areas
- Drive contact/appointment conversions
- Showcase success stories as social proof
